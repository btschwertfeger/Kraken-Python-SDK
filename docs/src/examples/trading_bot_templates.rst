Trading Bot Templates
=====================

The `python-kraken-sdk`_ is perfectly suited to design automated trading algorithms. To facilitate the start here directly
for spot trading as well as for the futures enthusiast a template is provided, which can serve as a basis for a dynamic trading algorithm.
trading algorithm. In both cases the websocket endpoints are used to capture the live data directly. In addition
REST clients are also integrated, so that their endpoints can be reached at any time.

For questions, feedback, suggestions for improvement or problems `python-kraken-sdk/discussions`_ or `python-kraken-sdk/issues`_ may be helpful.
